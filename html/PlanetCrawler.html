Planet Crawler GameUnity 3D C# Personal Project

Planet Crawler is a game made in Unity3D C# solo by Michael Morel. The objective of the game is to claim as many planets as possible. This is done by having a higher Attack Power (top left number) than the Planet Power of the planet you are attacking. A taken over planet will turn dark and will produce Attack Power for you as an income.

Play Planet Crawler!
When a planet is clicked you can see it's Population/Current Season/Planet Power. When your Attack Power exceeds the selected Planets' Power an Attack Button will appear. When Attacked, A planet is claimed by the player and the Planets' Power is subtracted from your Attacking Power, however this difference is made up quickly.

What Makes Planet Crawler Special

Everything in this game is randomly made with every instance of the game. This means that each planet is completely unique in every reload of the game, this includes the planets position in space, color, size, Population, Current Season, and Power. This game was made to show a skill I have that would be valuable to a team. I decided to show off my skills at random, unique, and intriguing gameplay.

The game has a randomness that makes every instance of the game different. If this concept was fully implimented it could give a game a massive amount of replayability.
