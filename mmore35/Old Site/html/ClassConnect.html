Class Connect: Modernizing Education SoftwareFlutter/Dart Group project

Class Connect is a flutter/dart web/IOS/Android app made by Michael Morel, Rohan Kadkol, Annabelle Kanchirathingal, Haron Temam, Chamyria Muse, and Jayme Eaton. This application was created to give a more modern alternative to course structure software. To get specifics, please check the SRS Document. This project was presented in front of a class of over 100 students and around 10 potential investors by the group in December of 2021.
